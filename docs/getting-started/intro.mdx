# Introduction

Frag Orders is a mission planning web app for multiplayer DCS World. Upload your `.miz` file, publish it to a hosted workspace, and your pilots can review the plan, sign up for slots, and edit their own waypoints and radio presets — all before the server starts.

## How It Works

Frag Orders parses your `.miz` file and extracts the mission data: flight routes, waypoints, threat rings, ground units, and radio presets. That data is published to a live planning workspace where your coalition can collaborate. When you're ready to fly, Frag Orders writes any planned changes — waypoints, laser codes, radio presets, kneeboards — back into the `.miz` and you download it.

:::note
Frag Orders stores a copy of your `.miz` file on its servers in order to process and serve mission data. Do not upload `.miz` files containing data you are not comfortable storing on a third-party server.
:::

## What You Can Do

**As a mission creator or editor:**

- Publish a mission and share it with your coalition via Discord
- Set radio presets, laser codes, and event timing for all flights
- Manage who can access and plan each coalition
- Generate kneeboards automatically from mission data
- Track pilot signups and approve slot requests

**As a planner or pilot:**

- View the full mission map with routes, threat rings, and drawings before the event
- Edit waypoints and radio presets for your assigned flight
- Sign up for a slot and brief directly from the publish
- Download a `.miz` with your plan already written in

## Getting Started

1. Log in with your Discord account at [fragorders.com](https://fragorders.com)
2. Create a Frag Order and upload your `.miz` file
3. Publish to Blue or Red coalition
4. Share the link with your pilots

From there, see [Creating a Frag Order](../creating-a-frag-order/overview) for the full walkthrough.
