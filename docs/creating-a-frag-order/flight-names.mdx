# Flight Names & Colors

By default, each flight on the roster shows the group name straight from the mission file, and its map color is assigned automatically. You can override how a flight is labeled and pick a custom color — useful when squadrons want their callsign shown instead of the raw `.miz` group name, or want a color that matches their kneeboard.

Overrides change the label and color used across the **map, timeline, and roster**. The underlying mission group name is never modified.

## The Flights Tab

Open the **Flights** tab on the Frag Order detail page. This is the owner's editing surface: every flight in the coalition is listed with an editable **Display Name** and a **Color** picker.

![Flights tab](/img/flights.png)

Type a name to override the label, or leave it blank to fall back to the mission group name (shown as placeholder text). Set a color, or leave it on **Auto** to use the automatically assigned palette color.

The coalition selector at the top right switches between Blue and Red — overrides are configured per coalition.

## Planner Overrides

Next to the coalition selector is the **Planner overrides** switch. This toggle is **owner-only** — editors and planners cannot change it.

| State | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Planner overrides off** | Only the owner can rename flights and set colors, from the Flights tab. |
| **Planner overrides on** | A group's [planners](./managing-access#group-access-rules) (and editors) can also set their own flight's name and color, directly from the roster. |

Owners can always edit names and colors regardless of the toggle. The switch only governs whether group-level planners are also allowed to.

## Editing from the Roster

When planner overrides are on, anyone who can edit a group can rename it and pick its color without leaving the **Aircraft** roster. Each flight you can edit shows a pencil icon and a color swatch next to its label.

![Roster with inline flight editor](/img/planner.png)

- Click the **pencil** to turn the label into a text input. Type the new name and press **Enter** or click away to save — the change commits on blur and the roster refreshes automatically. Press **Escape** to cancel, or clear the field to remove the override.
- Click the **color swatch** to open the picker and choose a color. Pick **Auto** to return the flight to its automatically assigned color.

:::note Overrides travel with the publish
Renames and colors are stored on the publish, not the mission file. Re-importing or updating the mission keeps your overrides intact, and they appear for every viewer of the Frag Order.
:::
